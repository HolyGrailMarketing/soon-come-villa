A Ballroom Designed for Unforgettable Moments

Whether you're planning a romantic wedding, a corporate retreat, or a milestone celebration, our fully air-conditioned ballroom offers the perfect blend of elegance and functionality. With space to comfortably host over 100 guests, the ballroom at Soon Come Villa is designed to impress — without ever feeling overwhelming.

Spacious. Elegant. Versatile.

Our ballroom was built with both beauty and practicality in mind. From polished finishes to neutral tones that complement any event theme, it’s a canvas ready to be transformed into your vision. With high ceilings, soft lighting, and seamless flow into the outdoor gazebo and lawn, the space feels as open as the occasion calls for.
‍
Planning a wedding ceremony under the gazebo, followed by a dinner and dance inside? Hosting a conference, award ceremony, or private gala? Our team is happy to assist you or work alongside your event planner to bring your celebration to life.

The Soon Come Villa Ballroom is available for single-day events or as part of a full villa rental. Let’s make something beautiful together.
